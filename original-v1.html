Which One Are You?

Eight patterns. Each one made sense once. Most people are running at least one — usually without ever naming it. Find yours.

The Achiever

Doing everything right and feeling dead inside. Outruns the emptiness with more — but the goal post keeps moving.

The Peacekeeper

Never says what she needs. Twenty years of swallowed words have built a wall of resentment she can't describe.

The Fixer

Solves everyone's problem so he never has to sit with his own. Feels most alive when someone needs him.

The Avoider

Doesn't start what she might not finish. Lives in a permanent state of almost. Avoidance as protection.

The Perfectionist

Standards impossible by design. If the bar can't be cleared, he can never truly fail — just never enough, yet.

The Pleaser

Learned love is conditional. Shapeshifts to be whoever's wanted — and has no idea what she actually wants.

The Controller

Doesn't trust it'll go well unless he manages every variable. Control is the only safety he's ever known.

The Drifter

Likable, capable, and in the same place for five years. Goes along with things. Disconnection disguised as flexibility.
